What We Do

Sanctuary

Providing a lifelong safe haven for horses in need, guided by consent and kindness. Our sanctuary nurtures healing, dignity, and natural connection.

Wellness

Championing consent-based equine wellness. We honor each horse’s voice, using holistic, empathy-driven care that fosters trust and transformation.

Education

Delivering outdoor education and experiential programs that connect humans and horses with nature, cultivating partnership, presence, and lifelong learning.

Programs

Healing Herd

Consent-based sessions pairing rescued horses with community members for mutual growth and wellbeing.

Equine-Facilitated Learning

Experiential education that deepens self-awareness and leadership through mindful partnership with horses and nature.

Open Sanctuary Days

Accessible field days for all ages—explore, learn, and connect with our herd and the great outdoors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is consent-based wellness for horses?
Consent-based wellness means horses actively participate in decisions about their care and activities. We use gentle, respectful practices that foster safety, trust, and healing for every horse.
How do horses come to Beyond Liberty?
Most are rescued from shelters, owner surrenders, or other urgent situations. Each arrives with a unique story and is welcomed into the Sanctuary with care and patience.
Who can join your education programs?
Everyone! Our programs are accessible to people of all ages, backgrounds, and experience levels—no previous horse or outdoor education needed.
How are donations used?
100% of donations fund essential horse care, wellness programs, education for the community, and sanctuary improvements, with transparency in all spending.
How can I volunteer or get involved?
Start by visiting our Get Involved page, where you’ll find forms for volunteering, sponsoring, or joining events.
